Empanadas

Description

What's better than some delicious homemade empanada dough? The dough is really easy to make and turns out great.
With the recipe I'm giving you, you'll get a dozen and a half of good-sized dough discs, perfect for stuffing them nice and plump.
This empanada dough can be used for baking or frying.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons of fine salt
  • 50 grams of butter or lard (pork or beef)
  • 250 cc. water

Steps

  1. In a small pot or saucepan, heat the water with the butter or lard. Once dissolved, turn off the heat and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, place the 500 grams of flour with the salt, make a well in the center, add the semi-beaten egg and the lukewarm water with butter or lard.
  3. Mix all the ingredients well, transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead until you achieve a smooth and tender dough. Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest for half an hour.
  4. Once the dough has rested, flour the surface and roll it out to the desired thickness, typically 2 to 3 mm thick.
  5. Cut out discs using a 10 cm diameter pastry cutter. Fill them to your liking.
